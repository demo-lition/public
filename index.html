About Demo-lition

We believe in openness, early feedback and sharing. Creators, users, customers, investors, friends, family, founders, entrepreneurs: just people willing to show their work and provide feedback so that better products (and services) can be built.

Building a product?

Prepare your product demo and show us in 7 minutes how we can use it and how we can get more value out of it. Or simply take a seat and listen to what others have to say.

Wanna join?

Come say hello and provide your feedback on industry-related products that you are interested in.

How we do it:

  • No Pitching. No Business Plan. No slides. No Videos. Demo your product as it was in front of your clients.
  • No labels. Just community. Treat your audience like people with feedback.
  • Come prepared with questions to the audience.
  • Feedback should be productive, regarding the product or the idea.
